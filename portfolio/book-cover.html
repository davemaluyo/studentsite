Book Covers

Fall 2020

Our task was to create three book jackets (a trilogy) using content from episodes of the podcast, Criminal by Phoebe Judge.

This project received the Pele Awards 2021 Best of Show in the category of: Sales Promotion, Publication Design - Cover

Client: Criminal Podcast
Service: Print, Illustration
Skills: Layout, Typography, Illustration

Book 1 - 527 Lime Street

Arson cases are unusual, this one changed books about fire science.

Book 2 - Hastings

An incident at a school where no one is hurt still leaves emotional scars.

Book 3 - Brownie Lady

Brownies can help people sleep, aid in appetite and comfort. They're just not your average brownies.

Design Process Video

Fall 2020

In this video project, I document what it's like to create a set of book covers using podcasts as the stories. Three episodes from the true-crime podcast 'Criminal' were chosen. Follow along with me to learn about my thought process and actions.

This project received the Pele Awards 2021 Gold in the category of: Elements of Advertising, Cinematography

Client: Criminal Podcast
Service: Advertising, Marketing
Skills: Video, Motion Graphic Design
